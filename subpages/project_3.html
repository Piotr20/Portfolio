Project description

We were asked by Arla to prepare a solution for their Danish customers that will allow them to put more data about their farming and help Arla towards 0 carbon emission future.

Purpose

We were asked by Arla to come up with ideas for solution that will allow farmers to put more data then they usually do around 100 questions, but to make the solution something more than just 4 time a year thing that the farmers will just go to when they have to.

Features

To give our farmers best expierience the dashboard is completely adjustable because everybody shows intrest in different aspects of farming. Many boxes as a defauly are able to be customized and present differen things.

Some people care more about money and it is fine for those people as well as for inforamtion other farmers the calculator section helps them how much more money they can make being a part of Arla Garden Plus
