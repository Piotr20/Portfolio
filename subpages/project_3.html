Project description

This project was our last project of 1st semester and it was an exam project. I didn't have much time to develop the concept, the main focus was to recreate a website of one of choosen companies. I chose Bose because of possibile design they might have. Even though at the time I didn't have best skills out there I focused mostly on visual indentity then structure.

Purpose

The purpose of this project was to make the website of one of the famous companies our own. It was our exam project so there wasn't much to experiment with, just playing it safe. Even though the structure of the website isn't the best I focused mostly on visuals to even learn possibly as much as possible.

Features

When we hear the word Bose we all think passion and that's what I tried to embrace on this website. But their goal is to sell their products, but custommers usually have doubts that's why many companies have articles clarifying many doubts for their clients

To manifest the values they represent I decided to throw some 'passionate' animations of drawing itself and photos of people with Bose products. Long history and tradition of the company is represented through their porudcts and approach to the clients and the website is suppose to support this ideas.
